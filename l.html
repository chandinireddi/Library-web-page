About

Libraries play a fundamental role in society as they are gateways to knowledge and culture. The resources and services they offer create opportunities for learning, support literacy and education, and help shape new ideas and perspectives required for a creative and innovative society. The task of library professionals is managing collection, organizing and issuing library resources, managing human resources, and other routines of the library. A library system(ILS), also known as a library management system (LMS), is basically resource planning for a library and keeps track of various divisions and activities of a library.
